ROTATE™
Foundations

The operator skills core curriculum. Where the physics of the free course becomes a trainable toolkit — the knowledge, skills, tools, and behaviors for running your own emotional and cognitive axes under load.

FormatSelf-paced operator curriculum
PrerequisitePsychological Safety (free)
Tuition$99

Psychological Safety teaches you why the human system behaves the way it does. Foundations teaches you what to do about it — the operator-level skills that turn the doctrine into capability you can run in real time, under real load.

It is built on the same engineering treatment as the rest of the ROTATE System: failure modes named precisely, skills drilled to recall, and the ROTATE™ and AIR™ protocols made operational rather than abstract. This is the curriculum for the operator who has read the specification and is ready to fly the aircraft.
