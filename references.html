The Research
Behind the System

The ROTATE™ System is a synthesis across eight fields that rarely speak to one another — aviation safety, cognitive science, attachment neurobiology, organizational culture. This is the body of work it stands on, organized by the territory each source maps.

The core sources directly informed the doctrine and the 2023 master’s thesis it grew from. Entries marked ∗ are convergent supporting evidence — work that did not directly inform the research but arrived independently at the same conclusions, included here as academic cross-validation.
